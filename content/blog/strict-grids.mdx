## Note

Strict grids are useful because they make the important decisions visible. When spacing, alignment, and hierarchy are controlled, the content can be more expressive without the whole page feeling loose.

For portfolio and business sites, a grid is not decoration. It is a way to help visitors scan quickly: what this is, what the work proves, where to go next, and how to make contact.
