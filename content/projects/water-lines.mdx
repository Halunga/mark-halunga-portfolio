## Public Clarity

Water work asks for a special kind of design: emotional enough to matter, sober enough to trust. The page structure uses strong hierarchy and direct language so visitors can understand the issue, the field evidence, and the shape of the work without needing institutional context.

The visual tone is calm and severe. No decorative ecology clichés. No soft green wash. The subject deserves a sharper frame.

## Long-Term Use

The same content model can hold reports, interviews, mapping notes, image sequences, and short public updates. It is built for slow accumulation rather than a single launch moment.
